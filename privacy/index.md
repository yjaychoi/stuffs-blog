Stuff of Thoughts uses [Kit](https://kit.com) to process newsletter subscriptions on `/subscribe/` and the compact form shown at the bottom of post pages.

Stuff of Thoughts uses [Cloudflare Web Analytics](https://www.cloudflare.com/web-analytics/) to measure aggregated traffic and page usage.

If comments are available on a page, they run through [giscus](https://giscus.app), which stores discussions in GitHub Discussions.

Third-party processors:

- [Kit Privacy Policy](https://kit.com/privacy)
- [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)
- [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)
- [giscus project](https://github.com/giscus/giscus)

For privacy requests and comment-removal requests, contact `privacy@stuffs.blog`.
